Problem Statement
Our goals were to find out what were the main causes of motor vehicle accidents in NYC? What would the frequency of accidents look like in the future? And how could we use this data to help people avoid motor vehicle collisions? The data was cleaned and visualized. Due to many unique locations, clustering was used to group close-by accidents, these clusters were then used to input data onto maps for better visualization. Then time-series analysis was conducted to predict future accidents.
